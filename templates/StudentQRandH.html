QR code and Hash Key are two options provided by College on your email for verifying the authenticity of a certificate. The QR code option involves scanning a code with a QR code reader, while the Hash Key option involves entering a unique code provided on the certificate. Both options lead to the same page, displaying the details of the certificate and confirming its authenticity. Our verification portal is committed to providing a secure and reliable way to verify certificates, while ensuring the highest level of privacy and data protection.
